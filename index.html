Stablecoin Analysis Project

About This Project

This project analyzes the stability of four stablecoins (USDT, USDC, DAI, and TUSD) from April 14, 2024, to April 14, 2025. Findings

Here are some key findings from the analysis:

  • USDC and DAI appeared to have almost no price variation (min/max price of 1.0000), possibly due to data rounding or cleaning.
  • USDT was stable with small fluctuations (min price of 0.9980, max of 1.0000).
  • TUSD showed significant deviations (min price of 0.9920, max of 1.0300), suggesting potential instability on certain days.
